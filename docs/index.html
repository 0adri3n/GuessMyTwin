How it works

01

Create or Join

Host a game room or join a friend's room using their URL. Set up your profile and choose a character set.

02

Get Your Character

Each player is secretly assigned a character. View all characters in the grid and prepare your strategy.

03

Eliminate & Guess

Click characters to eliminate them. When you think you know your opponent's character, hit the Guess button to win.
